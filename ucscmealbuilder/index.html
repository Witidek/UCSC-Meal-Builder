What makes this a beast of an app

01.

Up-to-date menus.

Menus are current with what's available in the restaurant.

02.

track meals/flexis.

Your meals/flexi blend will update when finalizing menu selections, or you can manually enter more meals/flexis.

03.

algorithmic.

Our algorithmic approach to selecting items to fit within budget allows you to maximize meals/flexi usage.

04.

optimized usability.

We've streamlined the app, from menu selection, calculation, to the checkout process.

05.

Save favorites.

Have a favorite meal? Simply save it, and when you're back, one tap away.

06.

elegant design.

Design is important, that's why we've tailored our app around design.

About us

We're the UCSC Meal Builder team. This project is for our UCSC CS 115, Intro to Software Design class. We're building this app using Android, and a PHP & MySQL database. Website is in HMTL/CSS. SCRUM is our method of team organization and project management. UCSC Meal Builder will be built in 3 sprints, overall lasting 10 weeks.
