ABOUT US

We're the UCSC Meal Builder team.

This app is for Richard Jullig's CS115, Intro to Software Engineering class. It's primary purpose is to teach us SCRUM team management techniques. Additionally it is meant to broaden our experience with various technologies, including but not limited to

  • Android
  • Java
  • PHP & MySQL
  • HTML/CSS
This app is intended to maximize a student's bang for his/her buck when purchasing from any UCSC restaurant. This app is going to be developed in 3 sprints, lasting for a total of 10 weeks. We have SCRUM meetings at least 3 times a week, with Thursday at 5pm being the primary SCRUM meeting with TA attendance.
